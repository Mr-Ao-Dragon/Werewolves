The Werewolf Mob is the default mob that represents the werewolf faction.
Werewolves spawn with different stats depending on a random internal level

Werewolves appear in two different form with different textures.

## Interactions
- If a werewolf bites a player without a faction, the player has the chance to be infected with [Lupus Sanguinem](../effects.mdx#lupus-sanguinem)
