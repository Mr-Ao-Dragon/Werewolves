## Plant Oil

Plant oil is the base for all other oils.

## Silver Oil

Increases sword damage against Werewolf Targets (Werewolf Player or Mobs).

### Silver Oil 1
- lasts 15 hits
- damage is multiplied by 1.125

### Silver Oil 2
- lasts 40 hits
- damage is multiplied by 1.2
