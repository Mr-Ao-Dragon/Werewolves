<Items title="Minion Charms" img={minionCharmTextures} source={minionCharmSource}>

When you are a Werewolf Lord you can bind other werewolves to your tribe by providing them with a Moon Charm. Once they accepted you as their leader you can level them up by giving them a appropriate upgraded Moon Charm depending on their needs. More Details on the [Werewolf Lord page](../werewolf/lord.md)
</Items>
