The Werewolf Expert is a Villager profession that has trades relevant for werewolf player.
The Job block for this profession is the [Totem Top](https://github.com/TeamLapen/Vampirism/wiki/Blocks#village-totem-topbase) block when the village is controlled by the werewolf faction
